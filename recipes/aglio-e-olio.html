How to make Aglio E Olio

Description:

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley
  • Peperoncino
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente
  2. Chop garlic, peperoncino, and parsley
  3. Put olive oil on a pan using medium low heat
  4. Put garlic and peperoncino in the olive oil and make sure it doesn't burn
  5. When spaghetti is al dente, put the spaghetti into the pan and add couple splashes of leftover water
  6. Stir until well done and add parsley on top and voila!
